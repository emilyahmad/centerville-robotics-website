Who We Are:

Centerville Robotics is a FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) team made up of 20 members from Centerville High School. Through FTC, we are determined to grow as a team, have fun, and leave a lasting impact on our community. At the core, we love FIRST Tech Challenge and look forward to every robotics meeting. Our passion drives our success!
